Reach

Overlooking Bristol’s Floating Harbour within the much acclaimed Finzels Reach development, this landmark building beneﬁts from excellent pedestrian, vehicular and waterway links to all parts of the city.

Brunel’s Temple Meads station is within a 12 minute walk while the recently opened Castle Bridge provides ready access to Cabot Circus, Quakers Friars and St Nicholas Market.

The Generator Building sits close to an array of bus routes (and the proposed new MetroBus routes) whilst Bristol’s water taxis and ferries can also be boarded from Castle Park.
